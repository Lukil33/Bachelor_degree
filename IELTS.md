🗓️ 2-Week IELTS Academic Study Plan (Goal: Band 7)
📅 WEEK 1 — Foundation + Skill Building

Focus: Understand the test format, build strategies, and identify weak areas.

Day 1 – Kickoff & Listening Fundamentals

📖 Watch or read an overview of IELTS Academic (understand band descriptors).

🎧 Do Listening Test 1 (Cambridge IELTS book).

Review why you got answers wrong — focus on synonyms, numbers, plurals.

🗣️ Speaking: Record yourself answering Part 1 questions (“home, study, hobbies”).

✍️ Writing: Read sample Task 1 + Task 2 Band 7 answers. Note structure.

Day 2 – Reading Skills: Scanning & Skimming

📖 Do Reading Test 1 (40 min).

Review every question → find proof in the text.

Practice TRUE/FALSE/NOT GIVEN sets.

🗣️ Speaking: Practice Part 2 cue card — “Describe a book or movie you enjoyed.”

✍️ Writing: Task 1 — summarize a simple chart or graph (150 words). Focus on comparisons.

Day 3 – Writing Task 2: Structure

Study essay structures (Agree/Disagree, Discuss Both Views, Problem/Solution).

Write one full essay (40 min).

Review with Band 7 checklist: clear opinion, topic sentences, linking words, examples.

🎧 Listening: Do Section 1–2 of a test (focus on prediction skills).

📖 Reading: Do one passage — timing yourself to 20 min.

Day 4 – Listening Practice + Grammar Review

🎧 Full Listening Test 2.

Focus on plural/singular, spelling, and transitions.

Grammar: Review common IELTS grammar (articles, prepositions, complex sentences).

🗣️ Speaking: Practice “Part 3 discussion” (2–3 min answers). Record yourself.

Day 5 – Reading Intensive Practice

📖 Reading Test 2 under timed conditions (60 min).

Review thoroughly.

✍️ Writing: Task 1 — process diagram or map (20 min).

🎧 Listen to English podcast (BBC Learning English / IELTS Listening material) for extra exposure.

Day 6 – Writing Integration Day

Write one Task 1 + one Task 2 under real timing.

Review vocabulary (use synonyms: increase → rise, surge, grow).

📖 Read The Economist or National Geographic article — summarize in 3 sentences (helps paraphrasing).

🗣️ Speaking: Practice two cue cards.

Day 7 – Mock Test #1

🧪 Full Listening + Reading + Writing in one sitting (no speaking).

Review errors carefully and note which section scores lowest — focus that next week.

Rest & light speaking practice in the evening.

📅 WEEK 2 — Exam Simulation + Targeted Improvement

Focus: Refinement, speed, and real-test timing.

Day 8 – Listening Accuracy Boost

🎧 Listening Test 3 — aim for 30+/40.

Review why you lost points (distractors? speed?).

Grammar: conditional & complex sentence review for writing.

🗣️ Speaking: Part 2 + Part 3 practice (record yourself; review for fillers and clarity).

Day 9 – Reading Precision

📖 Reading Test 3 (60 min).

Focus on NOT GIVEN vs FALSE differences.

✍️ Writing: Essay (Problem/Solution) — 40 min.

Revise linking words: However, Consequently, In contrast, As a result.

Day 10 – Writing Day

Task 1: Table or graph (20 min).

Task 2: “To what extent do you agree or disagree?” (40 min).

Review Band 7 writing descriptors:

Task achievement

Coherence & cohesion

Lexical resource

Grammar accuracy

Day 11 – Speaking Simulation

🗣️ Do a full speaking mock test with a partner or online.

Record yourself and self-evaluate:

Fluency: did you pause too much?

Range: did you use advanced vocab or idioms naturally?

🎧 Listening short practice + Reading 1 passage (maintain rhythm).

Day 12 – Mock Test #2 (Full Test)

Take a complete IELTS test (Listening + Reading + Writing).

Self-mark using official answer keys / sample responses.

Identify weakest area — that’s your focus for Days 13–14.

Day 13 – Focus Fix Day

If Listening weak → practice Sections 3–4 with notes.

If Reading weak → timed passage practice.

If Writing weak → rewrite one essay with corrections.

If Speaking weak → practice 10 Part 2 cue cards in a row (1 min each).

Day 14 – Final Mock + Relax

Do a final mini-test (Listening + Reading) in the morning.

Light writing revision (review templates).

Relaxation speaking practice in the evening (chat naturally with a friend or online partner).

Early sleep, hydrate, and go confident.
